Biography

Yusuf Olamide Kewuyemi is an astute researcher with a strong passion for food processing technologies, ensuring safe, nutritious, and healthy food. His expertise extends to existing, emerging, and novel techniques, with a keen interest in integrating Fourth Industrial Revolution (4IR) approaches, such as 3D food printing, big data, deep learning, and machine learning, to drive sustainability in food systems. His work supports key Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), including zero hunger (SDG 2), good health and well-being (SDG 3), economic growth (SDG 8), innovation (SDG 9), and responsible consumption and production (SDG 12).

Dr. Kewuyemi holds a National and Higher National Diploma in Food Technology with Distinction from Moshood Abiola Polytechnic, followed by a Master’s degree in Tourism and Hospitality with Distinction and a Ph.D. in Food Technology from the University of Johannesburg. His contributions include publications in top-quartile-rated journals, such as Critical Reviews in Food Science and Nutrition, Innovative Food Science and Emerging Technologies, Journal of Cereal Science, International Journal of Food Science and Technology, and Scientific Reports (Nature). Additionally, he holds a filed patent in two countries and was awarded the 2023 Innovation Award by Mühlenchemie GmbH & Co. KG, Germany, recognizing his groundbreaking work in food science.

Beyond food technology, he is a passionate learner with a keen interest in technology and innovation. His curiosity about how things work evolved into a full-fledged passion for solving real-world problems using technology. He actively explores interdisciplinary connections between food science and advanced technological innovations to enhance efficiency and sustainability.

Dr. Kewuyemi is also deeply committed to service projects, facilitating academic discussions, mentoring colleagues, volunteering in research departments, and reviewing papers for esteemed journals and institutions. Continuously learning and adapting, he remains at the forefront of scientific advancements, driven by curiosity and a dedication to innovation.
